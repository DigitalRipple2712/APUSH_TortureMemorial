What was the Burge Scandal?

Jon Graham Burge (December 20, 1947 â€“ September 19, 2018) was a decorated Vietnam War Veteran as well as a detective. He was accused of torturing over 100 African American Chicagoans between 1972 and 1991. He was the commander of the Torture Squad, a group of officers who's main goals were to extract confessions from the accused. Most of the so-called accused were African American while Burge was white. Some agree that racism played a large role in this scandal. While it was not directly stated, it can be inferred.


Major Event Timeline:

2018 - Burge passes away 2 days after the trial of another convicted police officer
